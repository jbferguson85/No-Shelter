about JB

No Shelter all started with a random status update from JB asking if anyone wanted to be in a Rage tribute band. Luke and Adam answered immediately, and things went from there. When JB is not playing with No Shelter, he can be heard playing with other groups in the Omaha area, such as Belles and Whistles. He is also a regular sub with the Omaha Symphony. He completed his Bachelor of Music technology in 2009.
